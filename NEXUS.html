About

BIM NEXUS is a multidisciplinary BIM services company that empowers MEP engineers, architects, and contractors with precise and coordinated digital models. We focus on delivering high-quality 3D modeling, clash detection, shop drawings, and full MEP coordination to streamline the construction process and minimize site conflicts.

Services

3D Modeling

Precise 3D BIM models for mechanical and electrical systems ensuring accurate design and coordination.

Clash Detection

Identify and resolve conflicts early between mechanical and electrical systems to avoid costly delays.

Shop Drawings

Detailed fabrication and installation drawings tailored for mechanical and electrical components.

MEP Coordination

Integration of mechanical, electrical, and plumbing disciplines into a coordinated BIM model for smooth project delivery.
