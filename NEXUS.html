About

BIM NEXUS is a multidisciplinary BIM services company that empowers MEP engineers, architects, and contractors with precise and coordinated digital models. We focus on delivering high-quality 3D modeling, clash detection, shop drawings, and full MEP coordination to streamline the construction process and minimize site conflicts.

Services

3D Modeling

We create precise 3D BIM models for mechanical and electrical systems to ensure accurate design representation and coordination.

Clash Detection

Identify and resolve conflicts between mechanical and electrical systems early in the design phase to avoid costly site issues.

Shop Drawings

Produce detailed fabrication and installation drawings specific to mechanical and electrical components, compliant with project standards.

MEP Coordination

Integrate mechanical, electrical, and plumbing disciplines into a single coordinated BIM model for seamless project delivery.
